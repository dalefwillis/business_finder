# Business brokers serving Madison, Wisconsin: A comprehensive acquisition guide

**The Madison area offers robust broker coverage** through approximately **25 qualified intermediaries** within a 2-hour radius, with the strongest concentration in Milwaukee/Brookfield and Green Bay. For acquirers seeking tech, manufacturing, or B2B businesses with **$300K–$1.5M cash flow**, the most credentialed and relevant brokers are Lake Country Advisors, CBS-Global, Cornerstone Business Services, and Sunbelt Wisconsin—all featuring IBBA certifications and explicit manufacturing/tech expertise. Madison itself has limited dedicated broker offices, but receives excellent coverage from regional firms.
